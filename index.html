About me

Hi! I'm an Italian photographer, developer, musician & student of ComputerScience @UniTn. I love technologies, graphic design, programming, numbers, network, algorithms, data and many other things! I'm a dreamer “interested in everything” and in the future I would like to launch an ICT start up.

I love Photography and art in general: my aim is to describe and document life through my personal point of view (and through the lens of my camera). I'm a guitarist for passion, reader for my knowing addiction and sometimes writer for hobby (blog coming soon, I don’t have the time for all that code at the moment!).

communication

I love the beauty and I insatiably look for it in people and things.
When it comes to branding, communication is everything: without the right image there is a risk that the product would not feel so attractive and live up to expectations.
From a couple of months I'm studying new communication techniques, business/management and economy/finance. Thanks to many collaborations I'm experimenting branding models that gather creativity, graphic design, marketing (specially web) and search engine optimization for offering products more cutting edge
